# JStudyRoom Document Viewing - Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### Q: What is JStudyRoom?
**A:** JStudyRoom is your personal collection of documents and study materials. It includes documents you've uploaded, purchased from the bookshop, or added as free content. You can view, organize, and study from these materials anytime.

### Q: How do I access my JStudyRoom?
**A:** After logging in, click on "My JStudyRoom" in the main navigation menu. You'll see all your documents displayed as cards that you can click to view.

### Q: What types of documents can I view?
**A:** JStudyRoom supports:
- PDF documents (most common)
- Image collections (JPG, PNG)
- Text documents
- Documents from bookshop purchases
- Uploaded files
- Free content you've added

### Q: Is there a limit to how many documents I can have?
**A:** Document limits depend on your account type:
- **Free accounts**: Up to 10 documents
- **Premium accounts**: Up to 100 documents
- **Unlimited accounts**: No limit

## Document Loading and Viewing

### Q: Why does my document show "Loading PDF content..." for a long time?
**A:** This happens when:
- **First-time viewing**: Document needs to be converted to viewable pages (1-3 minutes)
- **Large documents**: Files over 50MB take longer to process
- **Server busy**: High traffic can slow processing
- **Network issues**: Slow internet affects loading speed

**Solution**: Wait patiently for first-time conversions. Subsequent views will be much faster.

### Q: What does "Converting document to pages" mean?
**A:** When you first view a document, our system converts it into individual page images for optimal viewing. This process:
- Happens automatically
- Takes 1-3 minutes for most documents
- Only occurs once per document
- Makes future viewing much faster

### Q: Why do some pages load faster than others?
**A:** Page loading is optimized for better experience:
- **First page**: Loads immediately
- **Next 2-3 pages**: Preloaded in background
- **Remaining pages**: Load as you scroll
- **Previously viewed**: Cached for instant loading

### Q: Can I view documents offline?
**A:** Limited offline viewing is available:
- **Recently viewed pages**: May be cached in browser
- **Downloaded documents**: Available offline if downloaded
- **Mobile apps**: Some offline capabilities
- **Full offline**: Not currently supported for all content

## Technical Issues

### Q: My document won't load at all. What should I do?
**A:** Try these steps in order:
1. **Refresh the page** (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
2. **Wait 2-3 minutes** for conversion to complete
3. **Clear browser cache** and try again
4. **Try a different browser** (Chrome recommended)
5. **Check internet connection** stability
6. **Contact support** if issue persists

### Q: I see a blank page instead of my document. Why?
**A:** Blank pages can occur due to:
- **Browser compatibility**: Try Chrome or Firefox
- **Cache issues**: Clear browser cache
- **Document corruption**: Try downloading original file
- **Network interruption**: Refresh and try again
- **Processing error**: Contact support for assistance

### Q: What browsers work best with JStudyRoom?
**A:** Recommended browsers (in order of compatibility):
1. **Google Chrome** (latest version) - Best performance
2. **Mozilla Firefox** (latest version) - Good compatibility
3. **Safari** (latest version) - Works well on Mac/iOS
4. **Microsoft Edge** (latest version) - Basic functionality

**Note**: Internet Explorer is not supported.

### Q: Why do I get "Network Error" messages?
**A:** Network errors typically indicate:
- **Internet connectivity**: Check your connection
- **Server maintenance**: Temporary service interruption
- **Firewall/VPN**: May block access to content servers
- **ISP issues**: Contact your internet provider
- **Browser settings**: Security settings too restrictive

## Performance and Optimization

### Q: How can I make documents load faster?
**A:** To improve loading speed:
1. **Use Chrome browser** for best performance
2. **Ensure stable internet** (minimum 1 Mbps)
3. **Close other browser tabs** to free memory
4. **Clear cache regularly** (monthly)
5. **Update your browser** to latest version
6. **Use wired connection** instead of WiFi when possible

### Q: Why does my browser become slow when viewing large documents?
**A:** Large documents use more memory because:
- **High-resolution pages**: Detailed images require more RAM
- **Multiple pages**: Browser caches several pages
- **Background processing**: Conversion continues in background

**Solutions**:
- Close other tabs and applications
- Use "Fit to Width" viewing mode
- Take breaks to let browser recover
- Consider upgrading device memory

### Q: Can I adjust the quality of document pages?
**A:** Quality is automatically optimized based on:
- **Network speed**: Lower quality on slow connections
- **Device capabilities**: Adjusted for mobile vs desktop
- **Document type**: Text vs image-heavy content
- **Zoom level**: Higher quality when zoomed in

Manual quality adjustment is not currently available.

## Mobile and Tablet Viewing

### Q: Can I view documents on my phone or tablet?
**A:** Yes! Mobile viewing features:
- **Touch navigation**: Swipe and pinch gestures
- **Responsive design**: Adapts to screen size
- **Portrait/landscape**: Rotate for better viewing
- **Offline caching**: Recently viewed pages available offline

### Q: Why do documents load slowly on mobile?
**A:** Mobile loading can be slower due to:
- **Cellular connection**: Use WiFi when possible
- **Device memory**: Close other apps
- **Battery saving**: May limit performance
- **Mobile browser**: Desktop browsers often faster

**Tips**: Use WiFi, close background apps, ensure good battery level.

### Q: Do touch gestures work for navigation?
**A:** Yes, supported gestures include:
- **Swipe up/down**: Navigate between pages
- **Pinch to zoom**: Zoom in/out on content
- **Double tap**: Quick zoom to fit width
- **Long press**: Access context menu (where available)

## Troubleshooting Specific Errors

### Q: What does "Document conversion failed" mean?
**A:** This error indicates:
- **File corruption**: Original document may be damaged
- **Unsupported format**: File type not compatible
- **Processing timeout**: Document too large or complex
- **Server error**: Temporary system issue

**Solution**: Try again in a few minutes, or contact support with document details.

### Q: I see "Access denied" when trying to view my document. Why?
**A:** Access denied can occur when:
- **Subscription expired**: Premium content requires active subscription
- **Account suspended**: Payment or policy issues
- **Document removed**: Content no longer available
- **Permission changed**: Access rights modified
- **Technical error**: System authentication problem

**Solution**: Check account status, verify subscription, contact support.

### Q: What does "Browser not supported" mean?
**A:** This message appears when:
- **Outdated browser**: Version too old for required features
- **JavaScript disabled**: Required for document viewing
- **Missing features**: Browser lacks necessary capabilities
- **Compatibility mode**: IE compatibility mode enabled

**Solution**: Update browser, enable JavaScript, try Chrome or Firefox.

## Data and Storage

### Q: How much storage do documents use?
**A:** Storage usage varies:
- **Original documents**: Count toward your storage quota
- **Converted pages**: Cached temporarily, don't count toward quota
- **Downloaded files**: Stored locally on your device
- **Browser cache**: Temporary storage, automatically managed

### Q: Are my documents backed up?
**A:** Document backup includes:
- **Original files**: Securely stored on our servers
- **Converted pages**: Regenerated as needed
- **Account data**: Regular backups maintained
- **User downloads**: Your responsibility to backup locally

**Recommendation**: Download important documents as personal backup.

### Q: How long are documents stored?
**A:** Storage duration depends on:
- **Active accounts**: Documents stored indefinitely
- **Inactive accounts**: May be archived after 2 years
- **Free accounts**: Subject to storage limits
- **Subscription content**: Available while subscription active
